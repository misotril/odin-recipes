Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a popular dish made by coating chicken pieces in a seasoned batter or breading and deep-frying them until golden and crispy. The exterior is crunchy, while the inside remains juicy and tender. It's often seasoned with a variety of spices, and can be served with sides like mashed potatoes, coleslaw, or biscuits. Fried chicken is beloved for its savory flavor and satisfying texture, making it a classic comfort food in many cultures.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Buttermilk

Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Marinate Chicken
  3. Dredge
  4. Fry & Enjoy
