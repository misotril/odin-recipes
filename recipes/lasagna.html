Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich, comforting layers of flavor. It typically consists of wide, flat pasta sheets stacked with a variety of fillings, all baked together into a hearty casserole. The most common version includes a meaty tomato sauce (often made with ground beef, pork, or a combination), a creamy ricotta cheese mixture (sometimes blended with spinach or other herbs), and a generous layer of mozzarella cheese. The dish is then topped with more cheese, often Parmesan, before being baked until bubbly and golden A timeless comfort food, lasagna is often made for family gatherings, holidays, or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Sugar
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodles
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Prepare the ricotta filling
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Let it rest
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
