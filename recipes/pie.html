Cherry Pie

Description

Cherry pie consists of a tender, golden-brown pie crust filled with a sweet, tangy cherry filling made from fresh or frozen cherries, sugar, and a thickening agent (like cornstarch or flour). The filling is typically juicy and slightly syrupy, with the natural tartness of the cherries balanced by the sweetness of the sugar. The crust, often made with butter and a pinch of salt, provides a crispy, flaky texture that contrasts perfectly with the soft fruit filling

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 2 sticks of cold, unsalted butter
  • 7 tbsp of ice water
  • 4 cups of fresh or frozen cherries
  • 1 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the pie crust
  2. Cut in the butter
  3. Add ice water
  4. Form the dough and chill
  5. Assemble the pie
  6. Enjoy
